# Job Search Goals

- Preferred locations: Toronto, Vancouver, Montreal, or remote roles in Canada and the United States.
- Preferred roles: MLOps Engineer, AI Infrastructure Engineer, ML Platform Engineer, Applied AI Engineer, Production ML Engineer.
- Preferred industries: AI infrastructure, developer tools, applied AI products, ML platforms, workflow automation, data systems.
- Seniority level: Mid-level to senior individual contributor roles with strong ownership.
- Compensation target: Not specified in this example dataset.
- Visa / work authorization constraints: Not specified in this example dataset.
- Must-have requirements: Production-facing engineering work, reliability concerns, deployment ownership, observability, debugging-heavy environments.
- Nice-to-have requirements: Agentic workflows, orchestration systems, memory/state handling, infrastructure automation, model-serving performance.
- Companies or sectors to avoid: Research-only roles without product or production ownership.
- Outreach style preferences: Specific, low-hype, engineering-first messaging with one concrete proof point.

## Evidence Base

### Clear role signal
- Strong interest in production AI systems
- Strong interest in ML infrastructure and platform work
- Preference for applied engineering over purely theoretical research

### Clear strengths
- Long-running automation systems
- LLM integration
- Reliability engineering
- Debugging and regression recovery
- Stateful workflows

## Guidance For The Agent

- Prioritize roles where the candidate can own systems end-to-end.
- Favor teams that care about reliability, deployment, observability, and production hardening.
- Use concrete technical proof points in outreach.
- Avoid inventing compensation, visa, or relocation claims.
